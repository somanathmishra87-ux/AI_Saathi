AI ka Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) ek aisi technology hai jisme computer ya machine ko insaan ki tarah sochne, samajhne aur decision lene ki ability di jaati hai. AI data se seekhta hai aur apne experience ke base par behtar result deta hai.

AI kisne aur kab banaya?

Artificial Intelligence shabd pehli baar 1956 me use kiya gaya tha. Is concept ko introduce karne wale scientist ka naam tha John McCarthy. Unhone AI ko ek aisi science bataya jisme machine ko intelligent banaya ja sakta hai.

AI kya hai?

AI computer ko insan jaise sochna, samajhna aur decision lena sikhata hai. Jaise voice assistant, face recognition, recommendation system etc.

AI se kya kar sakte hai?

  • Resume banana
  • Study aur homework help
  • Job interview preparation
  • Business ideas aur planning
